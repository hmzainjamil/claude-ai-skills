# Market Proposal — Client Proposal Generator
## Triggers: market proposal, client proposal, write proposal, proposal template, send proposal, pitch deck

## PROPOSAL STRUCTURE
1. Executive Summary (1 para) — what you'll do + expected outcome
2. Problem Statement — their specific pain, quantified
3. Solution — your approach, tools, timeline
4. Deliverables — exact list of what they receive
5. Timeline — week-by-week milestones
6. Investment — pricing tiers (3 options)
7. Social Proof — 2-3 relevant case studies
8. FAQ — top 3 objections answered
9. Next Steps — clear CTA (sign here / book call)

## PRICING ANCHORING
Always show 3 options: Good / Better / Best
Price ratio: 1x / 2x / 3.5x
Most clients choose middle — that's your target

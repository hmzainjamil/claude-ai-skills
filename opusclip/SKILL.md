# OpusClip — AI Video Clipping & Repurposing
## Triggers: opusclip, clip video, repurpose video, short clips, viral clips, tiktok clips, reels from video

## WHAT IT DOES
Turns long videos (podcast, webinar, talking head) into 10-30 viral short clips
Auto-captions, hook scoring, viral potential rating

## WORKFLOW
1. Upload long video to OpusClip (opus.pro)
2. AI identifies top hooks, key moments
3. Exports: vertical 9:16 (TikTok/Reels) + square 1:1 (LinkedIn)
4. Add to UGC pipeline or post directly

## INTEGRATION WITH UGC AGENCY
- Record 1 long UGC video → OpusClip → 20+ short clips
- Pair with arcads-claude-code for AI actor versions
- Meta Ads Manager upload → A/B test hooks

# Legal Review — Contract & Agreement Checker
## Triggers: legal review, contract review, nda review, terms review, agreement check, legal check

## REVIEW CHECKLIST
- [ ] Parties correctly identified
- [ ] Payment terms clear (net days, late fees)
- [ ] Scope of work specific (no scope creep language)
- [ ] IP ownership clause — who owns deliverables
- [ ] Termination clause — notice period
- [ ] Liability cap — capped at contract value
- [ ] Governing law — jurisdiction specified
- [ ] Confidentiality — duration defined
- [ ] Non-solicitation — if present, duration reasonable
- [ ] Auto-renewal — opt-out mechanism present

## RED FLAGS (escalate to lawyer)
- Unlimited liability clause
- Perpetual license grants
- Unilateral amendment rights
- Mandatory arbitration in unfavorable jurisdiction

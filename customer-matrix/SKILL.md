# Customer Matrix — ICP & Audience Segmentation
## Triggers: customer matrix, ICP, ideal customer, audience segment, target customer, buyer persona

## ICP TEMPLATE
Industry: [vertical]
Size: [employees / revenue]
Pain: [top 3 problems they pay to solve]
Budget: [$X/mo they spend on this category]
Decision maker: [title, seniority]
Where they hang: [LinkedIn groups, subreddits, events, newsletters]
Trigger events: [what makes them buy NOW — funding, hiring, pain spike]

## AUDIENCE SEGMENTS (Meta Ads)
Cold: interests + lookalike 1-5%
Warm: video viewers 50%+, website visitors 30-day
Hot: add-to-cart, checkout initiated, email list

## FOR UGC AGENCY NICHE TARGETING
Supplements: founders 28-45, high cortisol keywords, biohacking groups
Skincare: women 24-40, beauty subreddits, dermatologist followers

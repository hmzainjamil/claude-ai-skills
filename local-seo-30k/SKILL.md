# Local SEO 30K — 22-Prompt Alventra Workflow
## Triggers: seo audit, do seo, run seo, seo workflow, seo system, seo reporting, seo architecture, seo of the business

## 4 WORKFLOWS — 22 PROMPTS TOTAL

### WORKFLOW 1 — Google Business Profile (Prompts 1-8)
1. GBP audit — NAP consistency, category, photos, posts
2. Review response templates (5-star, 3-star, 1-star)
3. Q&A seeding — 10 questions + authoritative answers
4. Service area optimization
5. GBP post calendar (weekly: offer/update/event/product)
6. Photo strategy (interior/exterior/team/products)
7. Citation building list (top 50 directories)
8. Competitor GBP gap analysis

### WORKFLOW 2 — On-Page SEO (Prompts 9-13, 21)
9.  Title tag + meta description for all pages
10. H1/H2 structure audit + rewrite
11. Local landing page template (city+service)
12. Schema markup (LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Review)
13. Internal linking map
21. Core Web Vitals checklist

### WORKFLOW 3 — Authority Building (Prompts 14-16, 22)
14. Backlink prospecting (local press, directories, partners)
15. Guest post outreach templates
16. Local PR angle ideation (5 story hooks)
22. Brand mention monitoring setup

### WORKFLOW 4 — Content + Tracking (Prompts 17-20)
17. Blog content calendar (12 months, local keywords)
18. Google Analytics 4 event tracking plan
19. Search Console coverage audit
20. Monthly SEO report template

## PRE-FLIGHT (run first)
Collect: Business name, address, phone, website, GBP URL, top 5 competitors, target city/service keywords
